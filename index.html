Growing Goodness: Our Approach to Farming

We own a small farm on the outskirts of Bangalore. On our farm, we follow Integrated Pest and Nutrient Management (IPNM) practices. We rely primarily on natural methods, but when absolutely necessary for the health of our crops, we use chemical inputs responsibly and in minimal amounts. Our goal is to ensure that our produce is both healthy and grown with care for the environment. Our motto is: “Grow the food we want to eat for a long and healthy life.”

More Details About our Produce

Black turmeric, botanically known as Curcuma caesia, is a rare herbaceous plant valued for its striking bluish rhizomes and potent medicinal properties. Native to Northeast India, black turmeric is revered in traditional medicine for its numerous health benefits. It is used for its purported analgesic, anti-allergic, and antimicrobial properties.

Note: Black turmeric should be consumed in moderation and with professional guidance, especially for those with medical conditions or pregnant women.

  • Powerful Anti-inflammatory
  • Rich in Antioxidants
  • Supports Respiratory Health
  • Aids Digestion
  • Boosts Immunity

Lakadong turmeric is known as Curcuma longa renowned for its exceptionally high curcumin content. It is native to the Lakadong region of Meghalaya, India. Lakadong turmeric is celebrated for both its culinary and medicinal virtues. Its robust flavor and deep color make it a favorite among chefs and home cooks.

Note: For best results, use Lakadong turmeric in moderation as part of a balanced diet. Consult a healthcare professional for specific health concerns

  • Exceptionally High Curcumin Content
  • Natural Immunity Booster
  • Supports Joint and Bone Health
  • Promotes Digestive Wellness
  • Enhances Skin Health

Black ginger is known as Kaempferia parviflora. It is a rare perennial herb distinguished by its dark purple-black rhizomes. Native to Thailand and parts of Southeast Asia, black ginger is highly regarded in traditional medicine for its wide-ranging health benefits. It is also used for its reputed aphrodisiac, anti-inflammatory, and adaptogenic effects.

Note: Black ginger should be used responsibly and in consultation with a healthcare professional, especially for individuals with existing health conditions

  • Boosts Energy and Stamina
  • Supports Vitality and Wellness
  • Promotes Healthy Blood Circulation
  • Rich in Antioxidants
  • Aids Cognitive Function
